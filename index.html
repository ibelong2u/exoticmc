Local Business Agent in UAE

We help international corporations establish a local presence in the United Arab Emirates by providing you with a local agent who can represent your brand in trade shows, client meetings, and business engagements. Our services include local credentials such as phone number and an office address, which helps portray a professional business identity to clients.

Once you are ready to formally enter the market, we walk you through the legal procedure to establish your business entity in UAE. Our experts will work with you to decide on the type on entity or a free zone establishment best suited for your goals, and ensure a complete hassle-free process from helping connect with a local sponsor, to opening a bank account.
